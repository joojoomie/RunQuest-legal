# 跑步英雄传（RunQuest）支持 / Support

_Effective date / 生效日期: 2026-07-10_

## 中文

需要帮助、报告问题、反馈建议，或询问隐私与订阅问题，请邮件联系：

**[support@astralogy.org](mailto:support@astralogy.org)**

请在邮件中尽量包含：

1. 设备型号与系统版本。
2. 跑步英雄传 App 版本。
3. 问题发生的页面或跑步流程。
4. 如果与购买或订阅有关，请不要发送完整支付信息；Apple App Store 负责处理付款与退款。

跑步英雄传不提供医疗建议。如你在跑步、心率、疲劳或健康状态方面有疑问，请咨询医生或合格健康专业人士。

我们会尽量回复支持邮件，但回复时间可能受时区、App Store 审核、设备问题复杂度和邮件量影响。购买、退款和订阅账单由 Apple 处理，相关问题可能需要通过 Apple 支持完成。

你发来的支持邮件（邮箱地址、内容与附件）如何被使用、保留多久、如何请求删除，见[隐私政策第 10 条](/)。

## English

For help, bug reports, feedback, privacy questions, or subscription questions, email:

**[support@astralogy.org](mailto:support@astralogy.org)**

When possible, include:

1. Your device model and OS version.
2. Your RunQuest app version.
3. The screen or workout flow where the issue occurred.
4. For purchase or subscription questions, do not send full payment information; payments and refunds are handled by the Apple App Store.

RunQuest does not provide medical advice. If you have questions about running, heart rate, fatigue, or your health, consult a doctor or qualified health professional.

We try to respond to support emails, but response times may vary depending on time zone, App Review timing, device-specific complexity, and email volume. Purchases, refunds, and subscription billing are handled by Apple and may need to be resolved through Apple Support.

For how your support email (address, content, attachments) is used, how long it is kept, and how to request deletion, see [Privacy Policy, section 10](/).
