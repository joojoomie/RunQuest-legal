# RunQuest 隐私政策 / Privacy Policy

_Effective date / 生效日期: 2026-06-25_

## 中文

RunQuest（"本 App"）尊重你的隐私。本政策说明本 App 如何处理数据。

1. **我们不收集、不上传、不出售你的个人数据。** 本 App 没有自有服务器，不做用户追踪，不含广告，不接入第三方分析。
2. **健康数据**：经你授权，本 App 通过 Apple HealthKit 读取跑步距离、心率、活动能量、体能训练数据，以及**体重与身高**，**仅在你的设备上**用于把运动转化为游戏进度并估算卡路里。健康原始数据不离开你的设备，不与第三方共享，绝不用于广告或营销画像。经你授权可写回一次体能训练记录。若未读到体重，RunQuest Plus 用户可在「我的冒险」**手动填写一次体重/身高**（仅本地保存、仅用于卡路里估算）。
3. **运动与定位**：在 iPhone 上，本 App 通过 CoreMotion（计步）与 GPS（定位）测量跑步速度与距离，**仅在前台跑步时、仅在设备本地**使用，不存储、不上传位置。
4. **运动洞察（RunQuest Plus 高级分析）**：最快 1km、配速趋势、疲劳点、心率漂移、同配速心率变化等分析，**完全在你的设备上**由本场与历史跑步数据计算；为此本 App 在设备本地保存最近跑步的配速/心率时间序列摘要。这些数据不上传、不出售、不用于广告，删除 App 即随之删除。
5. **订阅（RunQuest Plus）**：RunQuest Plus 是通过 Apple App Store 的**自动续期订阅**，付款与续期由 **Apple** 处理——本 App **看不到也不保存你的支付信息**；订阅状态仅通过设备上的 StoreKit 判定。Plus 只解锁运动洞察、便利与外观，**绝不**出售战力、提高掉率或更改跑步进度，**定价与任何健康数据无关**。可随时在「设置 › Apple ID › 订阅」管理或取消。
6. **Apple 服务**：App Store 购买、订阅管理和 Game Center 排行榜由 Apple 提供，并受 [Apple 隐私政策](https://www.apple.com/legal/privacy/) 与 [Apple Media Services 条款](https://www.apple.com/legal/internet-services/itunes/) 约束。
7. **Game Center**：若你使用排行榜，本 App 会向 Apple Game Center 提交**派生的游戏数据**（本季最高关卡、战力、像素外观编码），由 Apple 按其隐私政策管理。不提交任何健康原始数据或位置。
8. **本地存储与同步**：游戏存档与运动洞察数据保存在设备本地；Apple Watch 与 iPhone 之间通过系统配对通道同步，不经过我们的服务器（我们没有服务器）。
9. **你的控制**：可在「设置 › 隐私与安全性 › 健康 / 定位服务」随时撤回授权（撤回后游戏仍可玩）。删除本 App 即删除其全部本地数据。
10. **儿童**：本 App 不面向 13 岁以下儿童定向收集数据。
11. **政策变更**：如本政策有重大变化，我们会更新本页顶部的生效日期并在此发布更新版本。
12. **联系**：如有隐私问题，请联系 [support@astralogy.org](mailto:support@astralogy.org)。

## English

RunQuest ("the App") respects your privacy. This policy explains how the App handles data.

1. **We do not collect, upload, or sell your personal data.** The App has no servers of its own, performs no user tracking, contains no ads, and integrates no third-party analytics.
2. **Health data**: With your permission, the App reads running distance, heart rate, active energy, workout data, and your **body weight and height** via Apple HealthKit, used **only on your device** to turn exercise into game progress and to estimate calories. Raw health data never leaves your device, is never shared with third parties, and is never used for advertising or marketing profiling. With your permission it may write back a single workout record. If no weight is found, RunQuest Plus users can **enter weight/height once** in "My Adventure" (stored locally, used only for calorie estimates).
3. **Motion & location**: On iPhone, the App uses CoreMotion (step counting) and GPS (location) to measure running speed and distance, **only while actively running in the foreground and only on-device**. Location is not stored or uploaded.
4. **Workout insights (RunQuest Plus analytics)**: Analyses such as fastest 1km, pace trends, fatigue point, heart-rate drift, and same-pace heart-rate change are computed **entirely on your device** from your current and past runs. To do this the App keeps on-device summaries of recent runs' pace/heart-rate time series. This data is never uploaded, sold, or used for advertising, and is removed when you delete the App.
5. **Subscriptions (RunQuest Plus)**: RunQuest Plus is an **auto-renewable subscription** through the Apple App Store. Payment and renewals are handled by **Apple** — the App **never sees or stores your payment information**; subscription status is determined on-device via StoreKit. Plus unlocks workout insights, convenience, and cosmetics only; it **never** sells power, boosts drop rates, or alters running progress, and **its price is not based on any health data**. Manage or cancel anytime in Settings › Apple ID › Subscriptions.
6. **Apple services**: App Store purchases, subscription management, and Game Center leaderboards are provided by Apple and are governed by [Apple's Privacy Policy](https://www.apple.com/legal/privacy/) and [Apple Media Services Terms and Conditions](https://www.apple.com/legal/internet-services/itunes/).
7. **Game Center**: If you use the leaderboard, the App submits **derived game data** (season-best stage, power, a pixel-appearance code) to Apple Game Center, governed by Apple's privacy policy. No raw health data or location is submitted.
8. **Local storage & sync**: Your save and workout-insight data live on-device; Apple Watch and iPhone sync over the system pairing channel and never through our servers (we have none).
9. **Your controls**: Revoke permission anytime in Settings › Privacy & Security › Health / Location Services (the game remains playable). Deleting the App deletes all of its local data.
10. **Children**: The App does not knowingly collect data from children under 13.
11. **Changes to this policy**: If this policy changes materially, we will update the effective date at the top of this page and post the revised version here.
12. **Contact**: For privacy questions, contact [support@astralogy.org](mailto:support@astralogy.org).
